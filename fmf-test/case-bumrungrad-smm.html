Boosting Bumrungrad International Hospital's Online Presence

  • Customer

    Bumrungrad International Hospital

    Southeast Asia's largest international hospital located in Bangkok. About 40% of patients are expats, tourists or medical travelers from 190 different countries

  • Strategic Goals

    Creating a content marketing strategy for social media

  • Solution

    Social networks have rapidly evolved into a vital component of everyday life and an essential tool for effective digital marketing. Recognizing the importance of social media in enhancing audience engagement and building trust, Bumrungrad International Hospital partnered with us to elevate their online presence. Our expertise in content management and strategic social media planning was instrumental in developing a comprehensive approach tailored to their needs.

    We focused on creating a dynamic content marketing strategy for their social media platforms, aimed at engaging a diverse audience and maintaining interest in their healthcare services. This approach was aligned with the emerging trends in medical tourism and the growing demand for accessible, quality healthcare information online.

    Formulation of Marketing Strategy

    In developing a robust social media marketing strategy for Bumrungrad International Hospital, we began with a comprehensive analysis of the competitive landscape in medical tourism, particularly focusing on the increasing number of patients seeking treatment abroad. We aimed to set realistic and attainable goals that resonated with our defined audience segments. Notably, the robust growth in medical tourism directed our focus towards the Middle East and Far East markets, identifying key demographics such as high-income individuals and those seeking high-quality medical care abroad.

    Our strategy emphasized audience segmentation, understanding that each group required a bespoke approach. We identified the primary age demographic as 25-55 years, with a core focus on individuals aged 30-50. Additionally, recognizing various levels of demand in medical tourism, we tailored our marketing efforts to engage different audience segments: from those already familiar with treatment in Thailand to those still exploring their options. Our approach involved not only highlighting the benefits of treatment in Thailand but also specifically guiding potential patients to choose Bumrungrad Hospital for their healthcare needs.

    Through this targeted strategy, we ensured that our social media efforts were not only reaching a broad audience but were also resonating with the specific needs and preferences of each segment. This meticulous planning and segmentation were pivotal in developing a successful social media marketing strategy that effectively positioned Bumrungrad International Hospital as a top choice for medical treatment in the competitive landscape of global healthcare.

  • Visual Design

    In the realm of social media, the visual design plays a pivotal role in capturing audience attention and conveying brand identity. For Bumrungrad International Hospital, it was crucial to align their social media presence with the hospital's esteemed brand image while also catering to the aesthetic preferences of their target audiences. Our approach involved creating a refined and sophisticated visual design for their official Facebook communities, characterized by minimalistic elements that resonate with these specific cultural tastes.

    We strategically employed design elements that not only reflected the hospital's high standards of professionalism and care but also were appealing and accessible to a diverse international audience. This visual alignment was essential in ensuring that the first point of online contact was both engaging and representative of Bumrungrad's commitment to excellence in healthcare services. Through this tailored design approach, we effectively strengthened the hospital's digital brand identity, enhancing its appeal to potential clients seeking premium medical services abroad.

  • Content strategy

    Our comprehensive approach to developing Bumrungrad International Hospital's social media content strategy began with a detailed analysis of competitors in the global healthcare sector. We scrutinized the social media presence of international medical institutions, identifying their strengths and weaknesses, particularly those catering to the Middle East and Far East markets. This analysis was crucial in understanding the landscape and positioning Bumrungrad Hospital distinctively.

    Our positioning strategies were carefully formulated to consistently convey the hospital's key messages to the audience. We focused on two main strategies:

    • Brand Positioning: This strategy was centered around increasing the audience's familiarity with Bumrungrad Hospital. We highlighted the hospital's wide range of services, its state-of-the-art facilities, and competitive advantages through engaging social media posts. This approach was designed to establish Bumrungrad as a leading healthcare provider in the minds of potential patients.
    • Benefit Positioning: In this strategy, we emphasized the unique advantages of seeking treatment at Bumrungrad Hospital. Beyond showcasing cost-effective solutions, we also highlighted emotional appeals and the exceptional quality of care provided. This strategy was aimed at creating a compelling narrative around the benefits of choosing Bumrungrad for medical needs.

    Content categorization played a vital role in our strategy, allowing for systematic organization and targeted delivery of our messages. The primary content categories included:

    • The service provides a distributor with the module depicting reports that can be uploaded.
    • Report list:
    • Basic information about Bumrungrad Hospital;
    • Detailed insights into the services provided;
    • Highlighting the advantages of choosing Bumrungrad Hospital;
    • Sharing news and updates;
    • storytelling and informational posts.

    Through this strategic approach to content creation and categorization, we ensured that every piece of content served a specific purpose, targeting different levels of audience demand and engagement. The result was a highly effective content plan, tailored to resonate across varied social media platforms and with diverse audience segments, reinforcing Bumrungrad International Hospital's position as a top destination for medical tourism.

  • Results

    Impactful Outcomes of Bumrungrad’s Social Media Strategy

    Our SMM efforts for Bumrungrad International Hospital led to significant improvements in their social media presence, measurable through various key performance indicators (KPIs). We observed a substantial increase in audience engagement, reflected in higher likes, shares, and comments across all platforms. The ROI from our SMM campaign was particularly notable, showing a significant positive return when comparing the investment in our services against the growth in inquiries and conversions via social media channels.

    In addition to ROI, other vital metrics like follower growth rate, engagement rate, and conversion rate from social media campaigns to actual patient appointments were closely monitored and showed substantial improvements. Our targeted content strategy led to a higher relevance score in social media ads, reducing the cost-per-click (CPC) and increasing the click-through rate (CTR), further enhancing the campaign's cost-effectiveness.

    We also leveraged advanced analytics tools. This data helped in refining our strategies, ensuring that our content and ads were consistently reaching and resonating with the intended segments. The overall digital footprint and brand awareness of Bumrungrad International Hospital in social media have significantly expanded, establishing it as a leading destination for medical tourism in the Middle East and Far East markets.
