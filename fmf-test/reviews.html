Finance director,
"KIA Motors"

"KIA Motors" would like to express gratitude to FMF Company for their high level of professionalism in the area of information technologies and business process automatization.

For the long-term of our cooperation, we have repeatedly noted your competence, efficiency, operability and the desire to carry out the work efficiently and duly.

Due to our necessity to digitize business trip processing we set a challenging task to FMF.

As a result, a great amount of work was delivered at all the stages: application for approval, automatic calculation of per-diem, setting limits, processing orders in ERP-system, processing offers from the agency for accommodation and tickets, processing agency reports on delivered business trips (verification, uploading, delivering, etc.). In the end, we received a convenient and user-friendly system.

On top of that, I would like to point out several features and advantages the system developed provide us with:

  • Creating different types of fields to apply for a business trip. Configuring individual and collective tariffs, statuses with access settings;
  • Possibility to add comments to any approval status. Flexible configuration of sending notifications;
  • Automatic agency report import from Excel files;
  • The module allows us to perform data exchange with SAP.

We are looking forward to furthering productive and fruitful cooperation with your company, and we recommend FMF Company to everyone as a reliable partner in the field of integrated automatization of business processes.
